# Gaokao Essay Quote Bank (300+ 古诗文名句)

> A bank of 300+ classical Chinese quotes, organized by theme, with source,
> meaning, and usage examples. Use this in Stage 2 (resource gathering) and
> Stage 5 (full-text writing).

## How to use this bank

1. Pick quotes that **directly serve your specific 立意** — not random impressive lines.
2. Use at most 3-4 direct quotes per essay (more is excessive).
3. Each quote must be **自然化用** (naturally embedded), not a sticker.
4. Format pattern: `XXX (作者) — meaning — use in essays about...`

| 文章长度 | 引用次数 |
|----------|----------|

| 1000字 | 3-4处 |
